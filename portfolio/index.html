My name is Samawel JABALLI


Hi there! I'm Samawel JABALLI from Regueb, Tunis. I currently Research student , with experience in python programming and modelling in machine learning, statistical inference. Familiar with Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, Keras, NLTK work as a Sof. I have two years experience creating software, and websites for both myself, and for clients. I have experience programming in many different languages such as C, C++, C#, Java, PHP and Python, however some of my favourite projects involve working in Django for python.

I graduated from Faculty of Sciences of Monastir, Tunis with the mention very well in Computer Science and also I'm a research student at Research Laboratory in Algebra, Numbers theory and Intelligent Systems, Monastir 

My graduation project was realized in the laboratory RLANTIS of the professor Mounir ZRIGUI which is part of the Natural Languague Processing (NLP). My project consists of the automatic classification of arabic tweets into two categories ( positif and negatif): it is the most important task in sentiment analysis. The major solutions proposed in the literature are based on the machine learning supervised on manually collected and annotated data. The proposed models are affected by the lack of annotated data since manual annotation is greedy in terms of time and effort. In this project, i proposed a newly collected corpus of arab tweets and a system that is also based on neural networks for the classification of sentiments to detect positive and negative opinions of arab tweets.
